ABOUT US

9ja Vehicles App is an application for all vehicle related matters. This brings together all who are proud owners of vehicles, be it cars, buses, trucks, tippers, trailers, heavy duty vehicles or small vehicles like keke, etc. This platform gives all vehicle owners that opportunity to relive their joy, when these vehicles were bought by posing or showcasing what they have got.

The happiness expressed for some were short lived, because of so many factors. The challenge of vehicle theft, accident, unskilled mechanics, fake spare-parts dealers, insecurity, lack of adequate knowledge to care, preserve and protect what we cherished, led to so much pain and leaking pockets. But, worry not, 9javehicles is here now

Why Choose Us?

9javehicles is designed technologically to cater for most of these needs, to showcase, preserve and protect all vehicles in Nigeria.

We are revolutionizing the way to track and recover every stolen vehicle in record time. First, every member on board must be a licensed driver, a company or belong to a security agency to benefit from this technology. Secondly, we are bringing onboard all expertise in vehicle tracking in Nigeria. Thirdly, we are bringing onboard every vehicle dealer and spare-parts/vehicle accessory dealers. Fourthly, all registered driving schools in the country are coming onboard. And more importantly, we are partnering with all law enforcement agencies in the country, to guarantee swift response to any red-flag alarm made.

What these means is that, there is a fresh-breath of newness in Nigeria. In line with our new anthem, 9javehicles thrives to promote integrity in our dealings with one another. No more harassment by security agencies, because we provide very easy way of verifying licenses and vehicle papers compliance. Therefore, training and re-training of security personnel, concerned with vehicles will be available for the smooth transition to be complete. We provide fast way of tracking any stolen vehicle within the country. We will curb the exploitation going on, through fake practitioners who parade themselves as vehicle dealers, spare-parts and other accessories dealers, inadequately trained mechanics- who damage vehicles without any consequences, trackers- who just feed fat on the misfortunes of vehicle owners, etc.

Visionary Focus

Besides bringing solution, to the varied ills in the vehicle industry as a whole, we hope to bring enough publicity to approved and diligent practitioners to enjoy enormous patronage through our vast coverage while, also bringing awareness to possible areas capable of turning the economy fortune of this country as both government and Nigerians would have a mouthful, as a result of in-formed decision making based on facts.

We look forward to see all fuel-filling station businesses, carwash businesses, all licensed drivers onboard, to enjoy the vast publicity and opportunities that abound in 9javehicles, as we showcase what we have, to make the most, this great country Nigeria has to offer.
